Awareness & Safety Guide

Steps to Take if Harassed
  • Move to a safe or public place immediately
  • Call emergency services if in danger
  • Write down details (time, place, description)
  • Report to police, HR, or security authorities
  • Reach out to trusted family or friends
Recognizing Types of Harassment

Verbal: Threats, slurs, unwanted remarks.

Physical: Assault, inappropriate touching.

Stalking: Following or monitoring without consent.

Cyber: Threats, bullying, sharing private info.

Workplace: Harassment at job or hostile work environment.

Your Legal Rights
  • Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act (2013)
  • Domestic Violence Act (2005)
  • Indian Penal Code Sections 354 & related provisions
  • IT Act for cyber-related crimes

You can file an FIR at any police station, regardless of where the incident took place.
